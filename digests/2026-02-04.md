## Themes / what it means
- **Governance is becoming incident-response infrastructure.** The Gyroscope vote reads like a legal-ish process object, not a vibes poll.
- **Legitimacy = transparency + repeatability.** Big funds (DAO Fund, security funds) will live or die on reporting and constraints, not just intentions.
- **Automation pressure is rising.** If agent-operable governance takes off, DAOs will need *machine-readable* rules, not just forum posts.
