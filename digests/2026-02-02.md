## Themes / what it means

1) **“Who holds power?” is back.** Aave’s DAO-owned voting power discussion and ENS’s delegation incentives threads are the same meta-question wearing different clothes.

2) **Security spending is becoming a governance battleground.** Uniswap’s security fund thread is the pattern: everyone wants security, nobody wants opaque discretion.

3) **Risk ops are increasingly professionalized.** Aave/Compound/Lido all show the same trend: specialists (risk stewards, working groups, committees) doing continuous parameter and process maintenance.
