## Top governance moves (what actually matters today)

### 1) ENS: treasury transfer that exposes an execution-readiness bug
ENS is voting on **[EP 6.32] [Executable] Transfer $2.5M USDC from Endowment to wallet.ensdao.eth**.
- Forum (primary): https://discuss.ens.domains/t/ep-6-32-executable-transfer-2-5m-usdc-from-endowment-to-wallet-ensdao-eth/21882
- Onchain proposal (primary): https://www.tally.xyz/gov/ens/proposal/107313977323541760723614084561841045035159333942448750767795024713131429640046

What it is: a **one-time internal transfer** (Endowment Safe → DAO timelock) to restore USDC needed to execute **previously-approved** transactions.

Why it matters: the timelock balance shifted between simulation and execution because Superfluid **AutoWrap** wrapped USDC to fund streams, leaving the timelock short at execution time. Expect follow-on work on a “cash + obligations” checklist before things become executable.

### 2) Arbitrum: DVP-based quorum (copyable if it works)
- Forum (primary): https://forum.arbitrum.foundation/t/dvp-quorum-for-arbitrumdao/29996
- Constitutional proposal (primary): https://arbitrum.proposals.app/8623ce84-e296-44b0-ba82-4d0cafa53673

Problem: quorum tracks **voteable supply**, which grows with unlocks even if tokens never get delegated → a future governance **freeze** risk.

Proposed fix: quorum as a **% of DVP**, with an absolute minimum in ARB. If Arbitrum normalizes this, other DAOs will cite it as the standard “liveness” upgrade.

### 3) Aave: multichain pruning continues
- ARFC (primary): https://governance.aave.com/t/arfc-focussing-the-aave-v3-multichain-strategy-phase-1/23954

Core move: freeze Aave V3 deployments on **zkSync, Metis, Soneium** (reduce governance + risk bandwidth spent on low-activity deployments). Thread also floats a policy bar for new chains: **$2M/yr revenue floor** guaranteed by the host chain.

### 4) Optimism: buybacks as governance policy
- Forum proposal (primary): https://gov.optimism.io/t/proposal-to-align-the-op-token-with-superchain-success/10527

Key details (from the post): 12-month program; **50% of incoming Superchain revenue** used to buy OP monthly; executed via an **OTC provider** + an execution dashboard.

The real debate: healthy “usage ↔ token” alignment vs governance optimizing for price-support optics over public-goods / ecosystem compounding.
