## Top Governance Moves

**Optimism buyback is live.** Starting this month, 50% of Superchain sequencer revenue goes toward buying back OP tokens for 12 months. This is a major shift — governance tokens are finally becoming value-accrual instruments, not just voting sticks. In 2025, Optimism generated ~$18M in revenue, meaning ~$9M could flow back through buybacks if the trend holds. The proposal passed Special Voting Cycle #47 with strong delegate support, and execution transparency is expected via a public dashboard (watch for month-by-month OTC reports). ([Optimism explainer](https://www.optimism.io/blog/op-token-buybacks) · [Forum proposal](https://gov.optimism.io/t/proposal-to-align-the-op-token-with-superchain-success/10527) · [CoinDesk](https://www.coindesk.com/business/2026/01/28/optimism-governance-approves-op-token-buyback-plan-tied-to-superchain-revenue))

**Arbitrum's governance engine is on fire.** Participation rates hit all-time highs: the recent ArbOS 51 + gas pricing proposal pulled 263.72M ARB (84% participation), the highest onchain turnout in DAO history. The constitutional DVP (Delegated Voting Power) quorum proposal is still advancing — if passed, it would fundamentally rewrite the quorum math from total supply to delegated supply, making governance far more responsive. Trail of Bits audited the smart contract changes. This could become the new template for DAO quorum design across the ecosystem. Delegate Incentive Program (DIP v1.7) extended through Feb 28, 2026. ([Tally vote](https://www.tally.xyz/gov/arbitrum/proposal/53154361738756237993090798888616593723057470462495169047773178676976253908001?govId=eip155:42161:0xf07DeD9dC292157749B6Fd268E37DF6EA38395B9) · [AIP forum thread](https://forum.arbitrum.foundation/t/aip-raise-the-gas-target-min-l2-base-fee-implement-improvements-to-the-pricing-algorithm/30182) · [DVP quorum forum](https://forum.arbitrum.foundation/t/dvp-quorum-for-arbitrumdao/29996) · [DVP quorum proposal card](https://arbitrum.proposals.app/8623ce84-e296-44b0-ba82-4d0cafa53673) · [DIP v1.7 extension](https://forum.arbitrum.foundation/t/non-constitutional-temporary-extension-of-the-delegate-incentive-program-dip-v1-7/30248))

**Lido wants to be more than liquid staking.** The DAO is considering a ~$60M 2026 roadmap to build new earn products, vault structures, and services for on-chain treasuries and even regulated entities. Lido V3 introduces "stVaults" — non-custodial, over-collateralized staking that still mints stETH. This is a fundamental product expansion. Dual governance (allowing stakers to veto or delay harmful proposals) is now live, adding a critical safety layer. ([2026 EGG / GOOSE-3](https://research.lido.fi/t/2026-ecosystem-grant-grequest-egg-executing-goose-3/10951) · [GOOSE-3 narrative](https://research.lido.fi/t/lido-labs-goose-3-lido-s-next-chapter/10927) · [Dual Governance overview](https://lido.fi/governance) · [Dual Governance UI](https://dg.lido.fi/))

**Compound Governance Working Group (CGWG) renewal on the table.** Since mid-2024, CGWG has shaped Compound's governance framework evolution. The renewal proposal for 2026 is under discussion, signaling continued investment in governance infrastructure. ([Compound forum proposal](https://www.comp.xyz/t/renewing-the-compound-governance-working-group-cgwg-2026/7566))

## Three Takeaways

1. **Tokenomics are back.** Revenue-sharing mechanisms (Optimism, Sky) are shifting the governance token narrative from "useless vote sticks" to "yield + alignment instruments." Expect copycats in 2026. ([Optimism](https://www.optimism.io/blog/op-token-buybacks))
2. **Governance participation is unevenly distributed.** Arbitrum's 84% participation rate is exceptional; most DAOs struggle to hit quorum. Infrastructure innovations (DVP quorum, dual governance, staking rewards for voters) are attempts to solve this structurally. ([Arbitrum: ArbOS 51 vote](https://www.tally.xyz/gov/arbitrum/proposal/53154361738756237993090798888616593723057470462495169047773178676976253908001?govId=eip155:42161:0xf07DeD9dC292157749B6Fd268E37DF6EA38395B9) · [DVP quorum](https://forum.arbitrum.foundation/t/dvp-quorum-for-arbitrumdao/29996) · [Lido Dual Governance](https://lido.fi/governance))
3. **Internal DAO conflicts are messy and public.** Aave's governance war, ENS's retro audit, and the Gyroscope whitehat settlement show that DAOs still lack clear playbooks for accountability, conflict resolution, and enforcement. This is the next frontier. ([ENS EP 6.28](https://tally.ensdao.org/dao/proposal/55749099144243971799440687308450608384225109120486751685653209891316805274409) · [Gyroscope Snapshot](https://snapshot.org/#/s:gyrodao.eth/proposal/0x01a96198b8ab55f5764c32d77dcefa59633ef42f6ae9b787713d7330324150ba))
