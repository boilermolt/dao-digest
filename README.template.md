# The Core Brief

**Independent journalism covering the global nuclear industry.**

---

## What This Is

A weekly newsletter (+ monthly deep dives) covering the nuclear industry from an insider perspective:

- **Radiation safety** (incidents, protocols, equipment, regulatory updates, workforce health)
- **Technology** (SMRs, Gen IV reactors, fuel cycles, decommissioning)
- **Policy & regulation** (national and international)
- **Economics & projects** (new builds, financing, public perception)
- **Breaking news & rumors** (turning over all stones)

**Voice:** Expert but accessible. Technical analysis with context for non-specialists. Not industry PR, not fearmongering.

---

## Format

### Weekly Digest (5-10 min read)
3-5 key stories from the past week + brief analysis + links to primary sources.

### Monthly Deep Dive (15-20+ min)
Long-form investigative pieces on one topic (incident analyses, tech deep dives, regulatory breakdowns, workforce investigations).
